# Aroma Buddy — Support

Welcome! This page helps you with common questions, restoring purchases, and contacting support.

## Getting Started
- Discover featured oils
- Create custom blends
- Organize with collections
- Recently added blends appear on your home screen

## Restore Purchases
If you previously purchased Premium:
1. Open Aroma Buddy
2. Go to the Premium menu (top-right) and tap “Restore Purchases”
   – or open the Premium screen and tap “Restore Purchases”
3. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID you used to buy Premium.

## Troubleshooting
- Premium still locked after restore:
  - Check your internet connection and try again.
  - Confirm you’re on the same Apple ID.
  - Restart the app and retry “Restore Purchases”.

- App crashes or unexpected behavior:
  - Restart your device and try again.
  - If the issue persists, email us with your iOS version and steps to reproduce.

## Data & Privacy
Aroma Buddy stores your blends on your device. Purchases are processed by Apple. If you contact us by email, we use your address only to reply to your request.

## Disclaimer
Aroma Buddy is for wellness and education. It does not provide medical advice.
